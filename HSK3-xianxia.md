# 第一章: 白猫与兄弟

山很高。树很绿。**林风**和**沈星**住在山上。他们是好朋友，也是兄弟。他们每天一起练习，一起吃饭，一起看星星。

<small>The mountain is very high. The trees are very green. **Lin Feng** and **Shen Xing** live on the mountain. They are good friends, and also brothers. They practice together every day, eat together, and look at the stars together.</small>

最近，山下的村子不安全。有人说，**黑风门**的人来了。黑风门是坏人组织。他们喜欢抢东西，打人，做坏事。他们学习坏的方法，身体变得很强，但是心很坏。人们叫他们**坏人修仙者**。村里的人很害怕。

<small>Recently, the village below the mountain is not safe. People say that members of **Black Wind Sect** have come. Black Wind Sect is a group of bad people. They like to rob things, hit people, and do bad things. They study evil methods, their bodies become very strong, but their hearts are very bad. People call them **evil cultivators**. The villagers are very afraid.</small>

一天早上，林风在门口看见一只猫。猫是白色的，眼睛很大。它不怕人。它走到林风脚边，轻轻叫了一声。林风笑了。他拿了一点水给猫喝。猫喝完水，用头碰了碰他的手。

<small>One morning, Lin Feng saw a cat at the door. The cat was white, with very big eyes. It was not afraid of people. It walked to Lin Feng’s feet and gave a soft meow. Lin Feng smiled. He got some water for the cat to drink. After the cat finished drinking, it rubbed its head against his hand.</small>

沈星走过来，看见白猫，也很高兴。他说：“这只猫很干净。我们叫它**小白**吧。”林风点头。小白跟着他们走进院子。它坐在太阳下，闭上眼睛，很安静。

<small>Shen Xing came over, saw the white cat, and was also very happy. He said, “This cat is very clean. Let’s call it **Xiao Bai**.” Lin Feng nodded. Xiao Bai followed them into the courtyard. It sat in the sun, closed its eyes, and was very quiet.</small>

---

林风和沈星一起练习用铁刀的方法。沈星的刀很快，林风的刀很稳。练完以后，他们坐在树下喝水。太阳照在身上，很暖和。

<small>Lin Feng and Shen Xing practiced the iron-dao method together. Shen Xing’s blade was very fast, while Lin Feng’s blade was very steady. After practicing, they sat under the tree and drank water. The sun shone on their bodies, and it felt very warm.</small>

沈星看着林风，说：“你的手有汗。我帮你擦。”

<small>Shen Xing looked at Lin Feng and said, “Your hand has sweat. I’ll help you wipe it off.”</small>

林风脸有点红，说：“谢谢。”

<small>Lin Feng’s face turned a little red. He said, “Thank you.”</small>

沈星没有马上放手。他轻轻擦掉他手上的汗。林风看着他的眼睛，心里很高兴。他们从小一起长大，感情很好。别人说他们是亲兄弟，其实他们只是好朋友。但是，他们心里都知道，对方最重要。

<small>Shen Xing did not let go right away. He gently wiped the sweat off his hand. Lin Feng looked into his eyes and felt very happy inside. They had grown up together since childhood, and their relationship was very good. Others said they were real brothers, but in fact they were only good friends. Still, both of them knew in their hearts that the other person was the most important.</small>

晚上，小白没有睡觉。它坐在屋顶上，看着月亮。它的眼睛突然发出一点光。那光很亮，像星星。但是林风和沈星没有看见。他们正在睡觉。

<small>At night, Xiao Bai did not sleep. It sat on the roof, looking at the moon. Its eyes suddenly gave off a little light. That light was very bright, like stars. But Lin Feng and Shen Xing did not see it. They were sleeping.</small>

第二天，老师叫他们去山下。老师说：“黑风门的人去了东边的村子。你们去帮忙。小心坏人。”

<small>The next day, the teacher called them to go down the mountain. The teacher said, “The people from Black Wind Sect went to the village in the east. Go and help. Be careful of the bad people.”</small>

林风和沈星拿上铁刀，准备出发。小白跳下屋顶，走在他们前面。它回头看了看他们，好像在说：“我带路。”

<small>Lin Feng and Shen Xing took their iron blades and prepared to set out. Xiao Bai jumped down from the roof and walked in front of them. It looked back at them, as if saying, “I’ll lead the way.”</small>

路上，风很大。树在摇。林风对沈星说：“我们一定保护村子。”

<small>On the road, the wind was strong. The trees were swaying. Lin Feng said to Shen Xing, “We must protect the village.”</small>

沈星点头。他看着林风，说：“我和你在一起。什么都不怕。”

<small>Shen Xing nodded. He looked at Lin Feng and said, “I am with you. I am not afraid of anything.”</small>

小白走在前面，尾巴轻轻摇。它不是普通的猫。她是天上的好女人，变成猫的样子，来到世界上。但是，现在只有风知道这个秘密。

<small>Xiao Bai walked in front, its tail swaying gently. It was not an ordinary cat. She was a good woman from heaven, taking the form of a cat and coming into the world. But for now, only the wind knows this secret.</small>

# 第二章: 刀与剑

他们走到东边的村子。地上有很多脚印。房子前面有火把。黑风门的三个人站在那里。他们穿着黑衣服，拿着长刀。脸很冷，眼睛很凶。村民躲在后面，不敢说话。

<small>They walked to the village in the east. There were many footprints on the ground. There were torches in front of the houses. Three people from the Black Wind Sect stood there. They wore black clothes and held long blades. Their faces were cold, and their eyes were fierce. The villagers hid behind them and did not dare to speak.</small>

林风跑过去，大声喊：“停下！”坏人转头看他。一个人笑了一下，举起刀冲过来。林风马上举起铁刀，挡住那把刀。刀碰到一起，发出很大的声音。沈星从旁边跳出来，踢中另一个人的腿。那个人倒在地上，大声叫。

<small>Lin Feng ran over and shouted loudly, “Stop!” The bad men turned to look at him. One person laughed a little, raised his blade, and charged over. Lin Feng immediately raised his iron blade and blocked the attack. The blades struck together and made a very loud sound. Shen Xing jumped out from the side and kicked another man’s leg. That person fell to the ground and cried out loudly.</small>

第三个坏人最强。他拿着黑色的剑，剑上有奇怪的光。他看着沈星，说：“你们打不过我们。”沈星站直身体，看着他的眼睛，慢慢说：“苦海无边，回头是岸。”坏人听了，脸色变了。他生气地挥剑，直接向沈星砍去。

<small>The third bad man was the strongest. He held a black sword, and there was a strange light on the sword. He looked at Shen Xing and said, “You cannot defeat us.” Shen Xing stood straight, looked into his eyes, and slowly said, “The sea of suffering has no end; turn back and reach the shore.” After hearing this, the bad man’s expression changed. Angry, he swung his sword and slashed directly toward Shen Xing.</small>

沈星侧身躲开。小白突然跳到剑上，用爪子拍了一下。黑剑掉在地上。坏人吓了一跳。林风马上用肩膀撞倒他。沈星快步走过去，把刀放在坏人脖子旁边。三个人都输了。黑风门的人跑进树林，很快不见。

<small>Shen Xing turned sideways and dodged. Xiao Bai suddenly jumped onto the sword and struck it once with her paw. The black sword fell to the ground. The bad man was startled. Lin Feng immediately rammed him down with his shoulder. Shen Xing quickly walked over and placed his blade beside the bad man’s neck. All three men lost. The people from Black Wind Sect ran into the forest and soon disappeared.</small>

打完了。村子安静下来。沈星收好刀，走到林风面前。他看到林风手臂上有一道小伤。沈星眉头皱起来，轻轻碰了一下，说：“你疼吗？”林风摇头，看着他的脸，心里很暖。他小声说：“没事。你在身边，我很放心。”小白坐在旁边，舔了舔爪子。她的眼睛闪了一下，像天上的月亮。没有人知道她刚才用了特别的力量。

<small>After the fight ended, the village became quiet. Shen Xing put away his blade and walked in front of Lin Feng. He saw a small wound on Lin Feng’s arm. Shen Xing frowned and gently touched it, saying, “Does it hurt?” Lin Feng shook his head. Looking at his face, he felt very warm inside. He quietly said, “It’s fine. When you are by my side, I feel at ease.” Xiao Bai sat nearby and licked her paw. Her eyes flashed once, like the moon in the sky. Nobody knew that she had just used a special power.</small>
